About WellnessWave

Empowering your journey to optimal health and wellness

Our Mission

To democratize access to evidence-based health and fitness information, providing tools and resources that empower individuals to take control of their wellbeing. We strive to cut through misinformation and make the path to a healthier lifestyle clear, sustainable, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their starting point.

Our Vision

We envision a world where preventative health practices are the norm, not the exception. Where individuals are equipped with the knowledge, motivation, and practical tools to make informed decisions about their physical and mental wellbeing. WellnessWave aims to be the catalyst for a global shift toward proactive health management and sustainable fitness habits.

Our Story

WellnessWave began with a simple observation: despite the abundance of health information available online, people were struggling more than ever to achieve their wellness goals. The problem wasn't a lack of informationâ€”it was too much conflicting, complicated, and often commercially-motivated advice.

Founded in 2022, our platform was created to address this gap by providing clear, science-backed guidance on nutrition, fitness, and mental wellbeing. We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable health information and practical tools that make wellness accessible and sustainable.

What sets WellnessWave apart is our holistic approach. We understand that true wellness encompasses not just physical fitness, but also nutrition, recovery, stress management, and mental health. Our integrated platform brings together these essential components in one user-friendly ecosystem designed to support your complete wellness journey.

Our Leadership

Mr. Tan Jun Yang

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in the health and wellness industry, Tan Jun Yang founded WellnessWave with a clear vision: to cut through the noise and provide people with evidence-based health solutions that actually work.

Prior to establishing WellnessWave, Jun Yang served as a health consultant for international fitness organizations and contributed to several research studies on sustainable fitness practices. His approach combines scientific rigor with practical, real-world applications.

"My goal is to transform how people approach their health. Too many are caught in cycles of extreme dieting, unsustainable workout regimens, and information overload. At WellnessWave, we're building a platform that meets people where they are and guides them toward lasting health with compassion, clarity, and credibility."
